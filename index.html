Genuine Assent Tools

Genuine Assent refers to an agreement between parties that is made freely and without any form of misrepresentation or duress. It ensures that all parties involved truly understand and agree to the terms of the contract.

TOOL ONE

The purpose of this tool is to assess and program for genuine assent. This tool may be useful for the following situations: (1) ascertaining whether genuine assent is present or not, (2) troubleshooting a current program, or (3) planning for the implementation of a new program.
